Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Best Ways to Play

There are essentially countless ways you can play Concentration! Besides setting up your own "house rules" related to keeping score and/or turn order, the "best" ways you can play this game are based around two factors: who's playing, and what sort of cards you are using. Perhaps you want to go head-to-head against a friend and see who messes up first in trying to turn over pairs? Or maybe you have a large group of people playing and see who can create the most card pairs before all the cards are flipped? You can even use specially-made cards that use a wider variety of potential paired sets, or they can be based on a particular theme based on other popular series from television or video games. The possibilities are endless!!!
